Our Story

Roots in faith,
songs in the ground

Judah Plows is a Christian Nepali worship band united by a single call: to surrender music to the Lord and let it become something that breaks open hard soil — in hearts, in communities, in the church.

The name comes from the tribe of Judah and the prophetic image in Hosea: breaking unplowed ground as an act of seeking God. That is what every song tries to do.

Drawing from Nepali musical roots and the global worship tradition, the band crafts original songs and covers that move between intimacy and declaration, between lament and joy.
